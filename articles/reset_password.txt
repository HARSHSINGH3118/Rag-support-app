To reset your password:

1. Click on the 'Forgot Password' link available on the login screen.
2. Enter your registered email address and submit the form.
3. You will receive a password reset email with a secure link.
4. Click the link in the email and follow the instructions to create a new password.

Important Notes:
- If you don't receive the email within a few minutes, please check your Spam, Junk, or Promotions folders.
- Make sure you're checking the correct email account that you used to register.
- If you're still unable to reset your password, please contact our support team at support@example.com for manual assistance.
